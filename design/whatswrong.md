# Whats wrong with current Local Line System

This is a summary of the current pain points with the Local Line storefront and admin workflows.

1) No reliable monthly CSA billing (requires manual resets of member billing cycles).
2) No built-in Herdshare agreement tracking; we rely on third-party tools and syncing.
3) Delivery areas can only be filtered by zip code, not by polygon/geo areas.
4) Subscriptions are designed for recurring boxes, not membership-style CSA billing.
5) Members cannot pause or cancel their own subscriptions.
6) No native product reviews.
7) Limited UI control (hard to add recipes, featured items, or seasonal content).
8) Reporting requires a complex, separate back-end to produce usable outputs.
9) Wix front end is decoupled from Local Line, creating an inconsistent experience.
10) Pricing and inventory are tied to price lists; we want role-based pricing (e.g., SNAP role tags per product).
